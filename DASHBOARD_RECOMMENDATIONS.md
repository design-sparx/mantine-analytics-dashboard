# Dashboard Recommendations

## Current Dashboards
- Analytics
- CRM
- Default
- E-commerce
- Finance
- SaaS

## Highly Recommended Additions

### 1. Marketing Dashboard
**Use cases**: Digital marketing agencies, social media managers, content teams

**Key metrics**:
- Campaign performance (CTR, conversions, ROI)
- Social media analytics (engagement, followers, reach)
- Email marketing stats (open rates, click rates)
- Content performance, SEO metrics
- Marketing funnel visualization

### 2. Healthcare/Medical Dashboard
**Use cases**: Hospitals, clinics, telemedicine, health tech

**Key metrics**:
- Patient appointments & waiting times
- Bed occupancy rates
- Medical inventory
- Doctor/staff schedules
- Patient satisfaction scores
- Health metrics visualization

### 3. Education/LMS Dashboard
**Use cases**: Schools, online learning platforms, training programs

**Key metrics**:
- Student enrollment & attendance
- Course completion rates
- Grade distributions
- Assignment deadlines
- Instructor performance
- Learning progress tracking

### 4. Real Estate Dashboard
**Use cases**: Property management, real estate agencies, rental platforms

**Key metrics**:
- Property listings & availability
- Sales pipeline
- Rental income tracking
- Maintenance requests
- Occupancy rates
- Market trends & pricing

### 5. IoT/Monitoring Dashboard
**Use cases**: Smart home, industrial monitoring, fleet management

**Key metrics**:
- Device status & health
- Real-time sensor data
- Alerts & notifications
- Energy consumption
- Geolocation tracking
- System uptime

### 6. HR/Employee Dashboard
**Use cases**: HR departments, team management, workforce analytics

**Key metrics**:
- Employee directory
- Attendance & leave tracking
- Performance reviews
- Recruitment pipeline
- Team productivity
- Training & certifications

### 7. Server/DevOps Dashboard
**Use cases**: System administrators, DevOps teams, hosting providers

**Key metrics**:
- Server health & uptime
- Resource usage (CPU, memory, disk)
- Application performance (APM)
- Error logs & monitoring
- Deployment status
- Network traffic

### 8. Logistics/Supply Chain Dashboard
**Use cases**: Warehouses, shipping companies, inventory management

**Key metrics**:
- Shipment tracking
- Inventory levels
- Supplier performance
- Delivery times
- Fleet management
- Warehouse operations

## Nice-to-Have Additions

- **Restaurant/Hospitality Dashboard**: Orders, reservations, kitchen operations, table management
- **Fitness/Gym Dashboard**: Member tracking, class schedules, trainer assignments, equipment usage
- **Event Management Dashboard**: Event planning, ticket sales, attendee management, venue logistics
- **Cryptocurrency Dashboard**: Portfolio tracking, market trends, trading analytics
- **Nonprofit Dashboard**: Donations, volunteers, campaigns, impact metrics
- **Booking/Reservation Dashboard**: Calendar management, booking analytics, customer management

## Top 3 Priorities for Maximum Impact

### 1. Marketing Dashboard
- Huge market demand
- Highly visual with diverse chart types
- Appeals to agencies, startups, and enterprises
- Complements existing analytics dashboard

### 2. Healthcare Dashboard
- Growing sector
- Underserved in open source templates
- High complexity showcases template capabilities
- Critical industry with specific needs

### 3. Education/LMS Dashboard
- Evergreen need
- Appeals to schools, universities, and edtech startups
- Diverse user roles (students, teachers, admins)
- Rich data visualization opportunities

## Implementation Notes

These three dashboards would:
- Cover major industry verticals not yet represented
- Demonstrate template versatility
- Provide value to diverse user bases
- Showcase different types of data visualization and interactions
